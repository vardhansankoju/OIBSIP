Sync Across Devices

VFitness works on your phone, watch, and fitness bands seamlessly. VFitness can sync fitness data seamlessly across phones, watches, and fitness bands. To achieve this, you'll need to set up VFitness on both your phone and watch, and potentially connect your fitness band to VFitness through a third-party app or the band's own app. This allows you to track your fitness progress on any of these devices and see the data synced across all of them.
